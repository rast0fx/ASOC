Our Elite Units

75th Ranger Regiment

Global Operations 24/7 Readiness

The 75th Ranger Regiment is one of five units within the Special Operations Command. The 75th Rangers Regiment is a light infantry unit that specializes in deployments and operations against high-risk enemies and foreign units. The Rangers Regiment is known for their special operations raids, forcible entry operations, and special reconnaissance.

Regiment Structure

The 75th Ranger Regiment consists of three elite Ranger Battalions:

1st Ranger Battalion

The elite battalion within 75th, focused on wargames and scrim/raids. They are the first to engage with enemies, deploying the best personnel 75th can offer. The battalion is organized into two specialized companies:

  • Hades Company - The scrimmage component of 1st Ranger Battalion
  • Ares Company - The wargames component of 1st Ranger Battalion

Comprised of the best personnel 75th can offer, they are the first to engage against enemy forces.

2nd Ranger Battalion

The wargames component of 75th, tasked with reconnaissance and more realistic missions. As a milsim unit, they are proficient in essential military skills including medical procedures, different formations, and callouts, all crucial for successful deployments. The battalion consists of two specialized companies:

  • Zeus Company - Special Operations Raid Force, tasked with hostage retrieval from enemy territory with support from Direct Actions
  • Hephaestus Company - Highly-Trained Mechanized Force, responsible for quick-insertion of troops, evacuation, and general transportation into deployment zones

Specialized in military simulation skills including medical procedures, formations, and tactical callouts.

Ranger Training Battalion

Ranger Training Battalion entails the hosting of all phases within the RASP (Ranger Assessment and Selection Program). Instructors will instruct candidates who passed our tryout and are currently processing through our program. Instructors will host RSDC 1 - 4 which include a JT, Land Navigation, Unit Specialization Classroom, and an Inspection. Instructors will also host RILC 1 - 5 which include a JT, Land Navigation, Inspection, and Supervision Phases. They also can host selections to Introduce new members to RASP. Head Instructors as well as Assistant Head Instructors grade RILC Final Exams.

42nd Orbital Operations Division

Multi-Terrain Operations Elite Intervention Force

The 42nd Orbital Operations Division is one of the United States Army's premier units, specializing in operations across extreme environments. From orbital deployments to arctic warfare and desert combat, they maintain readiness in all conditions. Their motto "Per Mare Per Terras Per Constellatus" reflects their capability to fight anywhere.

Global Intervention Battalion

The Global Intervention Battalion is the main combative battalion in the 42nd Orbital Operations Division. This battalion holds two companies, which both hold two platoons which all specialize in different aspects. We are the backbone to all of 42nd OOD, and house a majority of all personnel

Companies:
  • Eclipse Company: The introductory company focused on acclimating new personnel and pushing them to become the best combative fighters. Contains Leo & Gemini Platoon.
  • Trident Company: Specializes in anti-air missions and medical aid. Contains Consano (medical) & Vulcan (anti-air) Platoon.
  • Nova Drop Force: The most elite combat force specializing in competitive raids, scrimmages, and wargame matches.

Orbital Readiness Battalion

The Orbital Readiness Battalion focuses on training and preparing personnel for orbital missions. With a team of experienced instructors and advanced facilities, trainees receive the knowledge and skills needed for operations in space. The programs cover various aspects of orbital missions, providing hands-on experience and practical training.

The goal is to equip everyone with the confidence and competence to carry out their duties in the unique environment of space and harsh, physical environments.

Companies:
  • Atlas Company: Specializes in rough terrain and survival of harsh environments. Hosts the Cold Weather Leaders Course for Arctic Tab qualification.
  • Helios Company: In charge of orbital training tryouts and phases. Awards the Army Parachutist Badge & Space Operations Badge.

Army Special Forces

Global Operations Elite Intervention Force

The Army Special Forces - also known as the Green Berets - are an elite fighting force at the forefront of all wars with insurgents. Tasked with securing the hearts and minds of the population and starting insurgencies, they are the first point of call for the Army Special Operations Command when they need a revolution stirred up somewhere or enemies neutralized in a civilian-inhabited area.

1st Special Forces Group

The 1st Special Forces Group is a highly elite unit within USAR, specializing in unconventional warfare and special operations. Members are trained for their specific roles and participate in raids, scrimmages, and tactical missions.

Alpha Company

Specializes in raids and scrimmages, excelling in competitive tactical engagements.

Bravo Company

Focuses on wargames, known for their advanced strategies and teamwork in various wargames.

10th Special Forces Group

The 10th Special Forces Group specializes in training and advising allied forces around the globe. With a focus on building capacity and enhancing operational effectiveness, their experts provide critical instruction in counter-terrorism, unconventional warfare, and tactical skills. Their mission is to empower partner nations through hands-on training and strategic guidance, fostering strong alliances and improving global security.

Whiskey Company

Whiskey Company focuses on hosting for alpha company, while also focused on being the most elite instructional unit when it comes to FPS. They not only host, but also evaluate alpha company candidates to ensure they are up to standards,

Yankee Company

Yankee Company focuses on hosting for bravo company, focusing on wargames. They make sure to maintain a elite status when it comes to wargames between instructional units. And also evaluate bravo candidates to make sure they are up to standards.

Task Force 121

The main milsim unit, made for players who seek realistic operations and excellence in everything we do. We serve as an elite group for all sorts of operations, leading in high-risk situations. We specialize in high-risk operations, including High Value Target Elimination, Hostage Rescue and more. Only those who prove themselves may join our lines.

John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School

Training Center Elite Training

The John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School - also known as JFKSWCS - provides the United States Army with the highest quality badge and leadership courses, such as the Ranger School or the Military Freefall Course.

Training Excellence

The John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School prepares soldiers for advanced and specialized roles, whether that be a high attitude specialist or a pinpoint accurate marksman. JFKSWCS Instructors are the most prestigious in all of the United States Army, with only the best-becoming instructors in JFK, achieving the green beret.

1st Special Warfare Training Group

The 1st Special Warfare Training Group is one of 2 primary groups within the John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School. The 1st Special Warfare Training Group handles some rudimentary US Army courses. this includes the Military Freefall Course, Pathfinder Badge Course, and the Expert Infantry Badge Qualification Course. The 1st Special Warfare Training Group contains one of the most disciplined, mature, and well-balanced instructors in the US Army. These instructors have great airborne skills, air traffic control skills, and many more.

Artemis Company

The Artemis Company is responsible for the divisions Airborne Courses, such as the Military FreeFall Course and Pathfinder Badge Course. These courses are categorical to host and are one of the few courses that John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School Personnel can take.

Spartan Company

The Spartan Company is responsible for John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School's expert courses, these courses are meticulously supervised. The company itself is due to it's nature in hosting expert courses such as the Expert Infantryman Course.

2nd Special Warfare Training Group

The 2nd Special Warfare Training Group is one of 2 primary groups within the John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School. The 2nd Special Warfare Training Group handles some of the most rigorous courses within the United States Army, such as the Ranger School Course, which offers those who pass the renowned Ranger Tab and the Marksman Qualification Course. The 2nd Special Warfare Training Group contains one of the most disciplined, mature, and well-balanced instructors in the US Army. These instructors have great airborne skills, air traffic control skills, and many more.

Trojan Company

The Trojan Company is John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School's sole recruitment company, tasked with recruitment and keeping retention in JFKSWCS. Trojan is also responsible for being there for Companies or Groups that are in need of help. While Trojan handles recruitment; it also is allowed to host the Military FreeFall Course of 1st Special Warfare Training Group.

Cyrus Company

The Cyrus Company is one of John F. Kennedy Special Warfare Center and School's leadership companies. Due to its nature of being able to host the divisions strict qualification courses, such as the Special Operations Diver Qualification Course, also known as Combat Diver Qualification Course. Cyrus Company also hosts JFKSWCS's Ranger School, which is one of the division's most strict course.

160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment

Global Deployment 24/7 Rapid Response

160th Special Operations Aviation Regiment (more commonly known as 160th SOAR, or just 160th) is the only aviation unit out of the five units in Army Special Operations Command. It comprises two battalions, namely Eagle and Nightstalkers. The 160th conducts special aviation operations and is relied on by other divisions from within the command to conduct operations in the most challenging circumstances that no other person dare fly into. They are expertly trained in night-time operations, and are ready for any and all situations.

Eagle Battalion

Consists of the best crewman of the skies. While handling their basic jobs of maintaining aircraft during and off flight, they also handle duties such as milsim, medical rescue, wargames and scrims. The battalion consists of two companies, Sierra, which handles all crewmen specific duties, and Charlie, which trains and prepares crewmen for their specialization.

Sierra Company

The primary crewmen unit, made up of three elite platoons. Zulu Platoon specializes in infantry operations, providing ground support and tactical expertise while also being one of our main scrim units. Inferno is the main crewmen platoon, handling tactical air operations including Close Air Support (CAS), rappelling, and helicopter repairs. Finally, Phoenix Platoon is the medical platoon, trained to provide critical medical support during operations.

Charlie Company

One of the most capable units in the United States Army, focused on developing and training new crewmen. They teach a wide range of professional skills, with a particular emphasis on air assault operations and working alongside helicopters in combined operations. The company's main mission is to train crewmen and instructors in the Special Operations School.

Nightstalkers Battalion

The piloting group which specializes in transportation and attack support. Operating mostly under the cover of night, we're mostly seen utilizing the transport and attack variant of the BH.

Alpha Company

One of the most elite flying units in the United States Army. They're known for their expertise in flying a wide range of helicopters, handling everything from attack missions to transport operations. Whether it's getting troops into hot zones or providing air support, Alpha Company's pilots are trained to handle any situation with precision and skill.

Flight School Company

The foundation of aviation within ASOC. It's where all new pilots start their journey. They focus on giving incoming flight candidates the skills and knowledge they need to become most elite pilots of the next generation, shaping the aviators of tomorrow.

Black Angels Company

The first MILSIM-focused unit in SOAR, combining realistic military simulations with top-tier aviation training. They're also known for their impressive precision aerobatic performances and airshows, demonstrating their skill and control in the skies. As one of the most elite aviation companies in USAR, they excel in both combat operations and high-performance flying.
